Self Identity - Business Card

December 04, 2015 in Branding, Logo Design, Print

Brief: To produce a business card and brand myself.
Tone: Fresh, new, simple, organic.
Idea: When the J is viewed upside down in an oriented position, a simplistic scene of a fishing pole is presented, complete with an arm, fishing pole and curved fishing line. The symbolic fishing pole shaped flipped J represents me as a fisher in search of a fish. This in turn can be interpreted as a recent graduate in search of a job.
